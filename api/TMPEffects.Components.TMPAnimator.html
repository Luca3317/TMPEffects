    Remarks

    One of the two main components of TMPEffects, along with TMPWriter.
    TMPAnimator allows you to apply animations to the characters of a component.
    There are three types of animations:



    You may control when the animations are updated by setting UpdateFrom to and calling UpdateAnimations(Single).
